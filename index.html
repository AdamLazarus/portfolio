Artificial Life Simulation

This is the project that I completed for my dissertation at university. It's an Artificial Life Simulation which models the evolution and flocking behaviours of organisms. The graphics are very simple - the modeled organisms are shown as a white dot, their prey is shown as a yellow dot and their predators are shown as red dots. The display at the bottom shows the 'Average Fitness' (the ability to survive) and population of the organisms. As the organisms evolve over time, average fitness should increase as the organisms become better at avoiding prey while gathering food.
